Accommodation

For our big day we have chosen Bjertop Slott (the Bjertorp castle/manor house) situated on the Vara plain.

We know many of our guests come from afar and the castle offers rooming for a subsidized prise of 950 kronor (~100 euro). You book your room for the wedding night at registration.

If you desire to stay an extra night before (or after) the wedding, this should be booked with Bjertorp directly. Please notify that you are staying for the wedding and you will receive the same rate for lodging and breakfast, 950 kronor. There is a package deal for a two course dinner if you arrive the day before the special occasion for 1325 kronor.

Directions

The wedding ceremony will be held in Fyrunga church situated about two kilometers from Bjertorp, where the festivities take place later in the evening.

By car

We recommend to take the car to Bjertorp as public transport does not get you all the way there.

From Gothenburg: Take the E20 towards Stockholm for 110 km. About 10 km after passing Vara, take a right towards Fyrunga / Bjertorp slott.

From Stockholm: Take the E20 towards Gothenburg (Swedish: "Göteborg") 390 km. About 30 km after passing Skara, take a left towards Fyrunga / Bjertorp slott.

With train

To get to the location with public transport, we recommend taking the train to Vara Station (12 km fron Bjertorp). If you need help to get from the train station (which is rather hard), please note this when registering and we will help you out!

By airplane

The closest airport is Landvetter flygplats (GOT), which is about 100 km. From there, you can either take the bus to the central station and go by train, or go directly from the airport by rental car.

By car, to get from the airport to the E20 follow the directions towards Härryda (not Göteborg), and then towards Hindås. Now after about 2km take a left turn onto a small road towards Härskogen. This will take you on a small road for about 10 km through a forest before you reach the E20. Follow the E20 for about 1 hour, and about 10 km after passing Vara take a right towards Fyrunga / Bjertorp slott.
